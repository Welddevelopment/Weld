FAQ

A few things to know.

Is weld. free for developers? v

Yes. Building your profile and joining early access is free. You only pay later if we ever add optional, premium tools for you.

How do developers get verified? v

You connect your Roblox account, and weld. pulls in your real experiences and public game data. That proof of work speaks louder than any self-written bio.

Can studios contact me privately? v

Yes. Studios can browse and match with you without posting a public hiring thread or exposing that they're looking.

Which roles are supported first? v

We're starting with the roles studios hire most: scripters, builders, 3d and 2d artists, UI/UX, VFX, animation, and design. More roles unlock as the network grows.

How does matching work for developers? v

weld. ranks opportunities for you by role, fit, rate, and availability. When you and a studio both show interest, we open a direct intro.

Why should developers join early? v

You get in front of studios first, show up higher in searches at launch, and help shape how matching works before everyone else arrives.

Is weld. free for studios? v

Early access is free while we onboard the first wave of teams. Studio pricing will roll out later, once you're already seeing value.

How are developers verified? v

Developers link their Roblox account so weld. can tie profiles to shipped games and public performance data. You see real work, not just promises.

Can studios hire privately? v

Yes. You can quietly browse, shortlist, and match with developers without posting a public LFP thread or signalling your plans to the whole server.

Which roles can studios hire first? v

At launch you'll find scripters, builders, UI/UX, VFX, 3d and 2d art, animation, and design. We'll expand into more specialties as demand shows up.

How does matching work for studios? v

weld. ranks developers for each role by fit, rate, and availability. When you and a dev both show interest, we open a focused intro instead of a cold DM.

Why should studios join early? v

You get first pick of verified talent, better visibility to developers, and a direct say in how the matching engine evolves around real studio needs.
