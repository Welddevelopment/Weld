Why dark for studios? Studios are buyers — they're evaluating, comparing, deciding. The dark ember hero signals authority and seriousness vs the energetic red of the developer side. Same brand, different personality. The red still appears prominently in the proof section where studios see verified talent — that's where the energy lands for them.
Same 4 colours, different rhythm. Toggling modes doesn't change the palette — it resequences which background leads. Developer = red-first (energetic, creative). Studio = dark-first (authoritative, decisive). The nav and footer anchor points stay consistent so the page never feels completely alien on toggle.
